()||<BIN>10</BIN>:Persica can expend Deployment Points to deploy AR Team Dummies on nodes under friendly control. When enemies approach, they will automatically attack and damage them.
()||<BIN>10</BIN>:Persica starts the battle with 3 Deployment Points and gains 1 more on the 2nd round. After that, Persica gains 1 more Deployment Point per round.
()||<BIN>10</BIN>:Deployment Points are represented by blue circle icons. Points which have not been used during one round carry over to the next, so please keep that in mind.
()||<BIN>10</BIN>:Different Dummies have different damage, range, attack interval, deployment duration and point costs. Please deploy them to suit the situation.
()||<BIN>10</BIN>:The Command Post's control terminal can be used to change the movement routes of the enemies on the upper levels, so please make full use of it in response to the battle conditions.
